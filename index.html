PROCESS

How I work

1. Quick call

We discuss your business, goals, audience and what you want your page to achieve.

2. Page audit

I review your current page and list what needs fixing first — bio, highlights, pinned posts, content gaps and design issues.

3. Content plan

I create a simple weekly structure so you always know what to post and why. No random content.

4. Design + setup

Templates, highlights, profile layout and visual style are built so the page looks clean and consistent.

5. Post + improve

We schedule content, track what performs, and adjust monthly to improve results.
